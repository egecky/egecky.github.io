About Me

I’m a fourth year PhD student in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at Purdue University, advised by Prof. Abolfazl Hashemi. I previously earned my B.Sc. in Computer Engineering and my B.Sc. in Mathematics from Boğaziçi University. My research interests include reinforcement learning and optimization for deep learning.
